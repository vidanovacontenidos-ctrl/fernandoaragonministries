Who is Fernando

A calling
to the nations

"God doesn't just call individuals — He calls families, cities and nations to be transformed by His glory."

Fernando Aragón is an apostle, author and international speaker based in Tucumán, Argentina. His ministry operates from Cristo Reina Church, where he exercises his calling with apostolic authority and passion for generations. Through conferences, books and mentoring, he has impacted thousands of lives across Argentina and the world.
